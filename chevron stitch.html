Chevron Stitch
(click on words/illustrations and then on images)
The Chevron Stitch Rug is the beginning of an idea for a larger rug and other things. I adapted the Chevron Stitch that is used to make crochet throw blankets because I was thinking about the difference between blankets and rugs, what each should look like/feel like and how one might be more architectural than the other. I will hopefully have more to say on that later.
